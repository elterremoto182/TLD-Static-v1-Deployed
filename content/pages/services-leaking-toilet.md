## What Causes Leaking Toilets and Faucets?

Understanding the causes helps prevent future problems:

### Common Causes:
- **Corroded valves** - Age and water chemistry corrode valve components
- **Problem with the o-ring** - Worn or damaged seals allow leaks
- **Worn out washers** - Daily use wears down washers over time
- **Improper installation** - Poor installation leads to premature failure

### Secondary Causes:
- **Mineral buildup** - Hard water deposits damage components
- **High water pressure** - Excessive pressure stresses fixtures
- **Age** - Older fixtures wear out and need replacement
- **Lack of maintenance** - Neglected fixtures fail faster

**Our experienced technicians identify the exact cause and provide the right solution to prevent recurring problems.**
