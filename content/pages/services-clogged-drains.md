## Common Causes of Clogged Drains

Our professionals have experience with all types of drain and sewer issues, including those caused by:

### Household Causes:
- **Grease** - Cooking grease that hardens in pipes
- **Hair** - Hair buildup in shower and sink drains
- **Soap** - Soap scum that accumulates over time
- **Food waste** - Food particles that get stuck in pipes
- **Mineral buildup** - Hard water deposits that reduce pipe diameter
- **Small objects** - Items accidentally dropped down drains

### External Causes:
- **Tree roots** - Invasive roots that grow into pipes
- **Pipe deterioration** - Aging pipes that collapse or crack
- **Ground shifting** - Soil movement that damages pipes
- **Improper installation** - Pipes installed incorrectly

**We identify the exact cause of your clog and provide the right solution to prevent it from happening again.**

### 1. How quickly can you fix a clogged drain?

Most simple clogs are resolved within 1-2 hours. More complex problems may require additional time, but we provide same-day service in most cases. Emergency service is available 24/7.

### 2. Will you damage my pipes during cleaning?

**No, absolutely not.** We use professional-grade equipment and techniques designed to clean your pipes safely without causing damage. Our video inspection ensures we use the right method for your specific situation.

### 3. Why do my drains keep clogging?

Recurring clogs usually indicate a deeper problem like pipe damage, tree root intrusion, or extensive buildup. Our video inspection identifies the root cause, and we provide solutions that prevent recurring problems.

### 4. How much does drain cleaning cost?

We offer **free estimates and consultations**. Our pricing is transparent with no hidden fees. The cost depends on the severity of the clog and the method needed, but early intervention is always more cost-effective than waiting.
