## Causes of Sewer Line Damage

Understanding the causes helps prevent future problems:

### Common Causes:
- **Lack of regular maintenance** - Neglected systems fail over time
- **Regular ground freezing and thawing** - Temperature changes stress pipes
- **Aging pipes** - Older pipes deteriorate and fail
- **Tree roots** - Invasive roots grow into pipes and cause damage
- **Buildup of hair, grease, and oil** - Clogs that lead to pipe damage

### External Causes:
- **Ground shifting** - Soil movement damages pipes
- **Construction damage** - Nearby construction can damage sewer lines
- **Corrosion** - Chemical reactions weaken pipes over time
- **Improper installation** - Poorly installed pipes fail prematurely

**Our advanced detection methods identify the exact cause and location of damage, allowing for targeted repairs.**
