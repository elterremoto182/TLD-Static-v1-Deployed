#### Signs of a Broken Sewer Line

The following are common signs that there is a sewer leak on your property:

- Strange noises coming from toilets
- Slow draining drains and bathtub
- Lack of water in your toilet
- Flooded yard
- Sewage smell in basement or other area of your property
- Low water pressure
- High water expenses

#### Causes of Sewer Line Damage

- Lack of regular maintenance
- Regular ground freezing and thawing
- Ageing pipes
- Tree roots growing into the pipes
- Buildup of hair, grease, and oil
