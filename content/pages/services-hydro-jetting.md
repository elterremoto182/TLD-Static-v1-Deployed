## Frequently Asked Questions

### 1. How does hydro jetting differ from snaking?

Snaking only clears the immediate blockage, leaving buildup on pipe walls. Hydro jetting uses high-pressure water to completely clean your pipes from the inside out, removing all buildup and providing long-lasting results.

### 2. Is hydro jetting safe for my pipes?

**Yes, absolutely.** When performed by professionals, hydro jetting is safe for all types of pipes including PVC, cast iron, and clay. We adjust pressure based on your pipe material and condition.

### 3. How long does hydro jetting take?

Most hydro jetting services are completed within 1-2 hours, depending on the length of your sewer line and the severity of buildup. You'll see immediate results.

### 4. Will hydro jetting prevent future clogs?

**Yes, hydro jetting provides long-lasting results.** By completely cleaning your pipes and removing all buildup, hydro jetting significantly reduces the likelihood of future clogs for months or years.

### 5. Can hydro jetting remove tree roots?

**Yes, hydro jetting can cut through and remove tree roots.** However, if roots are extensive, you may need additional services. We'll assess your situation and recommend the best solution.

### 6. How much does hydro jetting cost?

We offer **free estimates and consultations**. Our pricing is transparent with no hidden fees. The cost depends on the length of your sewer line and severity of buildup, but hydro jetting often saves money compared to repeated drain cleaning calls.

### 7. Do I need a camera inspection before hydro jetting?

We recommend a camera inspection first to assess your pipe condition and identify problem areas. This ensures hydro jetting is the right solution and helps us target problem areas effectively.

### 8. Are you available for emergencies?

**Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency hydro jetting services.** If you have a severe clog, sewer backup, or urgent drainage problem, call us immediately. We understand that drainage problems can't wait.
