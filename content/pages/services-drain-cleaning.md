## What Causes Clogged Drains?

Understanding the cause helps prevent future problems. We regularly handle clogs caused by:

### Common Household Culprits:
- **Grease and Oil** - Cooking grease that solidifies in pipes
- **Hair** - Hair buildup in bathroom drains
- **Soap Scum** - Residue that accumulates over time
- **Food Particles** - Food waste that shouldn't go down drains
- **Paper Products** - Toilet paper and "flushable" wipes that don't break down
- **Foreign Objects** - Items accidentally dropped down drains

### External Causes:
- **Tree Roots** - Roots that invade and clog sewer lines
- **Pipe Corrosion** - Aging pipes that deteriorate and catch debris
- **Bellied Pipes** - Pipes that sag due to ground shifting
- **Pipe Breaks** - Cracked or broken pipes that collapse

**Our video inspection identifies the exact cause, so we provide the right solution the first time.**

### 3. Will drain cleaning damage my pipes?

**No, absolutely not.** We use professional-grade equipment designed for safe, effective drain cleaning. Our video inspection helps us choose the right method for your specific pipe type and condition.

### 4. Can you prevent clogs from coming back?

**Yes, we specialize in main sewer line cleaning.** We use video inspection to locate the blockage, then employ professional equipment including hydro jetting to clear even the toughest main line clogs, including tree roots.
